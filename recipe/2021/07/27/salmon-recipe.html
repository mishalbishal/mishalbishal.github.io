Baked salmon recipe

Jul 27, 2021

This recipe takes about 40 minutes total.

Ingredients: Salmon, Soy sauce, Extra virgin olive oil, Salt, Pepper, Paprika

  1. Start preheating the oven to 425Â°F.
  2. Cut salmon into reasonable serving sizes.

  1. Add soy sauce and extra virgin olive origin to top and bottom. You can rub it in with your hands.
  2. Add salt, pepper, and a dash of paprika.

  1. For easier cleanup, coat a pan with Aluminum foil and place the spiced salmon inside.

  1. By this point, the oven should be ready. Place inside for 16 minutes.
  2. Enjoy with some side dish*.

* Preparation of side dish is left as an exercise for the reader.
